# HR Policy Agent — topics

## Topic 1: Leave policy

Trigger phrases:
- how many days leave do I get
- apply for annual leave
- maternity leave policy
- sick leave rules
- carry forward leave
- leave balance

Conversation flow:
1. Ask which type of leave (Annual / Sick / Maternity / Other)
2. Provide the relevant policy details
3. Share steps to apply via the HR portal
4. Confirm if the employee needs further help
